# Margin

Apply margins of a specified size and direction.

Available combinable variations:

- `0`, `xxs`, `xs`, `s`, `m`, `l`, `xl` sizes
- `neg` for negative margins
- `top`, `right`, `bottom`, `left`, `x`, `y` directions
- `@s` and `@m` breakpoint suffixes
