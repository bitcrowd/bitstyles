# Hidden

## Hidden from everyone — you, search engines, screenreaders…

Fully hides an element & its children — it will be taken out of the document flow, and will not be read by google or screenreaders. Available at every breakpoint defined in `$bitstyles-hidden-breakpoints`.

<Canvas>
  <Story name="Hidden">
    {`
      <p>Paragraph 1 content</p>
      <p class="u-hidden">Paragraph 2 content</p>
      <p>Paragraph 3 content</p>
    `}
  </Story>
  <Story name="Hidden (small only)">
    {`
      <p>Paragraph 1 content</p>
      <p class="u-hidden@small-only">Paragraph 2 content</p>
      <p>Paragraph 3 content</p>
    `}
  </Story>
  <Story name="Hidden (large)">
    {`
      <p>Paragraph 1 content</p>
      <p class="u-hidden@large">Paragraph 2 content</p>
      <p>Paragraph 3 content</p>
    `}
  </Story>
