# Navbar

A top-level navigation container, with two sections separated to the left & right. The left-hand side is commonly used for a logo and links, the right-hand side for user account menu and global search.

Give the active link the `aria-current="page"` attribute.

## Handling large numbers of links

This navbar is only intended to hold a few link to the main sections of your site. If you absolutely must have more links than can be easily handled, the navbar will scroll. Once your list of top-level sections becomes very long, you may want to look at the [UI/Sidebar](/docs/ui-navigation-sidebar--sidebar) pattern instead.
