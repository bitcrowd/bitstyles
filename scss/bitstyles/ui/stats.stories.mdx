# Stats

Highlight a few statistics that will be important to the user as soon as they arrive on the screen. Displayed in 3 columns when screenspace allows, it’s best to highlight no more than 3 stats.

Highlighting a few statistics presents us the opportunity to give context and increase rapid understanding. This example indicates the direction of change over time for each statistic.
